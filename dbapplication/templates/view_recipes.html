Famous Veg Fast Food Recipes

Easy, delicious, and purely vegetarian dishes you can try at home!

Pav Bhaji

  • Boil potatoes, peas, carrots. Mash well.
  • Sauté onions & tomatoes with butter, ginger-garlic, pav bhaji masala.
  • Add mashed veggies, cook well, adjust salt & spices.
  • Toast pav buns with butter. Serve hot with lemon & onion.

Masala Dosa

  • Make dosa batter from rice & urad dal (fermented).
  • Prepare potato filling with mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric & boiled potatoes.
  • Pour batter on tawa, spread thin, drizzle oil, add potato masala.
  • Fold & serve with coconut chutney & sambar.

Chole Bhature

  • Soak & boil chickpeas. Prepare masala with onion, tomato, ginger-garlic & spices.
  • Add chickpeas, simmer until thick.
  • Make bhature dough (maida, yogurt, baking soda). Rest 2 hours.
  • Roll & deep fry. Serve hot with chole, onions, pickle.

Veg Pizza

  • Spread pizza sauce on base. Top with veggies like capsicum, onion, tomato, olives.
  • Generously add cheese.
  • Bake at 220°C for 12-15 mins.
  • Sprinkle oregano & chili flakes. Serve hot!

Dahi Puri

  • Crack puris, fill with boiled potatoes & chickpeas.
  • Top with yogurt, green chutney, tamarind chutney.
  • Sprinkle sev, coriander & chaat masala.
  • Serve immediately for crunch!
